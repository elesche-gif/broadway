Phantom of the Opera

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Summary: The Phantom of the Opera is a musical set at the Paris Opera House, where a disfigured musical genius known as the Phantom terrorizes the opera company while secretly mentoring a young soprano named Christine Daaé. The Phantom falls in love with Christine, but her heart belongs to her childhood friend, Raoul. The story follows the escalating drama as the Phantom's obsession turns to jealousy and rage, leading to him kidnapping Christine and creating chaos at the opera house.
