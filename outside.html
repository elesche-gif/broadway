The Outsiders

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Summary: The Outsiders is a Broadway musical adaptation of S.E. Hinton's novel about a 1960s rivalry between the working-class "Greasers" and the affluent "Socs" in Tulsa, Oklahoma. The story follows the introspective Greaser Ponyboy Curtis as he navigates themes of friendship, loss, identity, and violence, with iconic moments from the book and film, like the rumble and the church fire, reimagined through a new musical score and powerful visuals.
