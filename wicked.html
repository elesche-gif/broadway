Wicked

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
Summary: Wicked: The Untold Story of the Witches of Oz is a Broadway musical that re-imagines the classic tale from the perspective of its witches. The story follows the unlikely friendship between the intelligent but misunderstood Elphaba (who has green skin) and the popular, beautiful Glinda. The narrative explains how one became the Wicked Witch of the West and the other Glinda the Good.
